About

We are a group of aviation enthusiasts interested in effective policy formulation for the drone ecosystem in India.

Charter

Civil Aviation Authorities worldwide have started rule making for unmanned aviation. There being several aspects to the field, we hope to take a holistic and informed approach to making recommendations to policy makers on such efforts in India. See also our work items, reference documents and plan.
